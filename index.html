Welcome to the Games!

FIZZBUZZ

Enter a positive number and get "fizz" if its divisible by 3, "buzz" if its divisible by 5, or "fizzbuzz" if its divisible by both 3 and 5.
Additionally, you can enter your own divisors and words!

FIBONACCI SEQUENCE

Enter a number n and get the sum of the n - 1 and n - 2 numbers in the fibonacci sequence.
Additionally, you can replace the values of 1 and 2 to get the sum of different numbers in the fibonacci sequence.

MIX OF BOTH

This game mixes the previous two games. Enter a number n and get the sum of the n - 1 and n - 2 numbers in the fibonacci sequence. If the numbers in the sequence are divisable by either divisors then their numbers will be replaced by the selected words.
Again, you can replace the values of 1 and 2 to get the sum of different numbers in the fibonacci sequence, as well as the divisors and selected words.
